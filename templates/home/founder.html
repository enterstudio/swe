A message from our founder

Thank you for your interest in Science Writing Experts.

My name is Nathan Hammond, and I created Science Writing Experts to help researchers who publish in English as a non-native language. At Science Writing Experts we help you eliminate language errors and give your writing a clear and natural flow, so that you can publish your work in top journals and reach the worldwide scientific community.

I am from the United States and received my PhD from MIT in 2010. Having studied Spanish, French, and Chinese, I understand the challenge of writing professional-quality work in a language that you did not learn as a child, even if you are quite fluent.

Please feel free to explore our website, register for an account, and request a free trial to see what Science Writing Experts can do for you.

Sincerely,
