About Science Writing Experts

Our mission

Scientific progress is a worldwide endeavor shared by all people and all nations. We believe that language should never restrict scientific dialog or prevent ideas from reaching an audience throughout the world.

Our mission is to remove language barriers for researchers so that today's greatest ideas in science can be heard by everyone in the global community.

Who we are

Science Writing Experts is headquartered in San Francisco. We were founded by a PhD graduate from MIT with a passion for science and language.

Our editors are fluent English speakers who are engaged in graduate research or have completed graduate degrees at top universities.

If you have any questions, we welcome you to contact us.
